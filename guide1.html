Beginner's Guide to AI

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology that allows machines to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. Today, AI powers chatbots, recommendation systems, smart assistants, and even self-driving cars.

What is AI?

AI refers to computer systems that can perform tasks requiring human intelligence such as problem-solving, language understanding, and decision-making.

Types of AI

  • Narrow AI – Designed for specific tasks
  • General AI – Human-level intelligence (future concept)
  • Machine Learning – AI that learns from data

How to Start Learning AI

Begin with basic concepts, explore AI tools, and practice using them daily.
